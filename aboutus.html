About Us

It's a drive to empower people to take charge of the nature around them. At Eksaath, we are uniting communities & championing sustainable solutions to protect our planet.

Our Activities

Our regular clean-ups at various locations, from beaches to tourist attractions, as well as eco-shiksha events embody our contribution to making the world a better place to live in.

Our Vision

Guided by the inspiring call of the warm sun on the horizon, we unite to forge an eco-friendly, sustainable world. Our collective commitment ensures a future where humankind and wildlife thrive in harmony, creating lasting impact for generations to come.

Our Mission

Our mission is to eradicate ignorance and exploitation by fostering an eco-friendly environment. We aim to achieve this through extensive tree planting in urban and rural areas, eliminating plastic pollution that endangers human, aquatic, and wildlife, and maximizing reforestation efforts to protect wildlife and connect people with nature. We engage communities at the grassroots level through education and awareness, empowering them to become stewards of the environment.

Our Motivation

Engage people at the grass roots level through education and awareness about the environment and empower communities involved in preserving and understanding nature by bridging the gap that separates us.

Our Story

Journey of Impact and compassion.

Founded in 2020 by Pragya Kapoor, Ek Saath - The Earth Foundation is an NGO working in the field of environment and sustainability with an aim to sensitize and build a sense of responsibility towards the environment among the locals. Learning to co-exist with nature and making it our biggest ally, Ek Saath wishes to become a movement and voice in our fight against climate change and accomplish sustainable development goals.

The Advisory

Diya Mirza - Advisory

Dia Mirza is an award-winning Actor, Producer, UN Environment Goodwill Ambassador & United Nations Secretary-General’s Advocate for Sustainable Development Goals.

Her exhaustive list of titles includes- Founder member of Wildlife Trust of India's Club Nature, Ambassador Wildlife Trust Of India, Ambassador for the Swachh Bharat Mission's "Swachh Saathi" program, and the first Indian Ambassador of Save the Children initiative amongst many others.

Kilaparti Ramakrishna - Advisory

Dr. Kilaparti Ramakrishna (Rama) is WHOI's new senior advisor to the President and Director on ocean and climate policy, Principal Policy Advisor to the Executive Director of United Nations Environment Programme (UNEP) in Nairobi.

Tom A. Dahlstrøm - Advisory

Mr. Tor A. Dahlstrøm worked for Norwegian Ministry of Foreign Affairs for nearly 40 years. Environmental protection has always been an important agenda for Tor, especially fighting plastic pollution.
